About Allure Beauty & Spa

Welcome to Allure Beauty & Spa, where luxury meets expertise. Founded by Onuoha Vivian, our spa represents years of passion and dedication to the beauty industry.

Located in the prestigious Floben Plaza at FHA Lugbe, we have created a sanctuary where modern beauty techniques blend seamlessly with traditional wellness practices. Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to provide you with an unforgettable experience of relaxation and transformation.

At Allure, we believe that true beauty radiates from within. Our mission is to enhance your natural beauty while providing a peaceful retreat from the demands of daily life.
